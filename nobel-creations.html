Nobel Creations (2018)

Nobel Creations is an annual project at Beckmans school of design. The brief is to make an creatively free interpretation of an assigned nobel prize, in the form of a gala dress. The Prize in economic science in memory of Alfred Nobel 2017 was assigned Richard H. Thaler, and the creation was a collaboration between Rita Roslin and Alida Bard.
